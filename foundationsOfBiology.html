Defining Life

Life can be defined in many ways, but there is a list of characteristics that scientifically defines life. The characteristics are:

It responds to the environment
- All living things detect change in their environment
- Ex. If you step close to a mouse, it will respond by running away.
It grows and develops
- All living things grow and develop
- Ex. A tadpole slowly grows into a frog
It produces offspring
- All living things are capable of reproduction
- Ex. A single cell splits into two daughter cells
It maintains homeostasis
- All living things are capable of maintaining a stable internal environment
- Ex. The human body shivers when it is cold to maintian a stable internal temperature
It has complex history
- All living things consists of large, complex molecules
- Ex. The human body has metabolism, which is the accumulated total of all the biochemical reactions occuring in a cell or organism
It consists of cells
- All living things consist of atleast one cell
- Animals and humans have different types of cells, but they still have cells

Principles of Biology

The Unifying Principles of Biology are what form the basis of biology. There are four principles of biology, which are:

Cell Theory
- The theory that all living things are made up of cells
Gene Theory
- The theory that the characteristics of living organisms are controlled by genes
Homestasis
- The idea in which a living thing maintains a stable internal environment
Evolution
- the change in the characteristics of living things over time

Interdependence of Living Things

All living things depend on other living things to survive. For example, all animals rely on plants to provide them oxygen. This is called interdependence. Interdependence comes in two forms:

Symbiosis
- A close relationship between organisms of different species in which at least one of the organisms benefits
- Ex. Birds eat parasites from a deer's fur
Competition
- A relatinship between living things that depend on the same resources
- Ex. Animals in a desert rely on water, but only the best get the little water that is in a desert
